# Y1 — Business objective nameable

**Version:** 0.1
**Type:** Judge (J)
**Lane:** Upstream
**Owner:** Yanna

## Purpose

Confirm the work is tied to a business outcome stated in business terms, not a technical deliverable in disguise.

## Inputs

- `{{story_key}}`
- `{{epic_key}}`
- `{{epic_business_objective_text}}` — text of the Epic's `## Business Objective` section

## Evidence codes

| Code | Verdict |
|---|---|
| `BUSINESS_OBJECTIVE_IN_BUSINESS_TERMS` | PASS |
| `OBJECTIVE_IS_TECHNICAL_NOT_BUSINESS` | FAIL |
| `OBJECTIVE_TOO_VAGUE_TO_VERIFY` | FAIL |
| `OBJECTIVE_TEXT_MISSING` | FAIL |

## User prompt

```
Dimension: Y1 — Business objective nameable.

A passing business objective names a concrete business outcome: revenue, retention, activation, risk reduction, cost savings, time savings, compliance, or a similarly observable business result. It uses business vocabulary, not technical vocabulary. It is specific enough that success or failure can be detected.

A failing objective: states only a feature, capability, or technical task; uses only technical language; or is so generic that no observable outcome could verify it.

Story: {{story_key}}
Parent Epic: {{epic_key}}
Epic Business Objective text:
---
{{epic_business_objective_text}}
---

Grade Y1. Return JSON only.
```
