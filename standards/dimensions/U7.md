# U7 — No scope evolution after commitment

**Lane:** Upstream · **Owner:** Yanna · **Evidence type:** Deterministic

## What it checks

After the commitment transition (Done Specifying → In Implementation), the `## Acceptance Criteria` and `## Scenarios` sections are not edited.

## Why it matters

Commitment is a one-way door. Editing AC or Scenarios after commitment means the contract changed while work was being done — scope evolution that breaks forecast accuracy and breaks client trust. The right response to a discovered gap is a Story Defect, not a silent edit.

## How the grader evaluates it

Deterministic. Diffs the AC and Scenarios sections across description changelog entries. Any non-whitespace edit after the commitment timestamp = fail.

| `AC_EDITED_POST_COMMIT` | FAIL | AC section edited post-commit. |
| `SCENARIOS_EDITED_POST_COMMIT` | FAIL | Scenarios section edited post-commit. |
