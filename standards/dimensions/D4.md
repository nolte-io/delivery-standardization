# D4 — All sub-tasks closed at Done Implementing

**Lane:** Downstream · **Owner:** Hector · **Evidence type:** Deterministic

## What it checks

When a Story transitions to `Done Implementing`, every one of its sub-tasks is in a Done status.

## Why it matters

"Done Implementing" means the work is complete and ready to hand to validation. Open sub-tasks at that transition mean work is being passed forward with known gaps. Either the sub-tasks were unnecessary (then delete them) or they represent real remaining work (then don't transition). This dimension enforces the choice.

## How the grader evaluates it

Deterministic. Snapshots sub-task statuses at the moment the parent Story transitions to `Done Implementing`. Any open sub-task = fail.

## Evidence codes

| Code | Verdict | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `ALL_SUBTASKS_CLOSED_AT_DONE_IMPL` | PASS | Every sub-task Done. |
| `OPEN_SUBTASK_AT_DONE_IMPL` | FAIL | At least one sub-task open. |
