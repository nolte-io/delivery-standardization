# U8 — Validation outputs are Acceptance or Story Defect only

**Lane:** Upstream · **Owner:** Yanna · **Evidence type:** Deterministic

## What it checks

While a Story is in the `In Validation` status, the only issues created under it are Story Defects (or the Story itself transitioning to Done). No new Stories, Tasks, or Bugs.

## Why it matters

Validation exists to confirm agreed behavior, not to discover new scope. Creating new work items during validation means validation is being used as a discovery phase — which breaks the predictability model.

## How the grader evaluates it

Deterministic. Tracks issues created during the time window the Story was in `In Validation`, linked to the parent Story. Non-Story-Defect creation = fail.

## Evidence codes

| Code | Verdict | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `VALIDATION_OUTPUTS_IN_CONTRACT` | PASS | Only Story Defects or acceptance. |
| `NEW_STORY_CREATED_DURING_VALIDATION` | FAIL | A new Story was opened during validation. |
| `NEW_TASK_CREATED_DURING_VALIDATION` | FAIL | A Task was opened. |
| `NEW_BUG_CREATED_DURING_VALIDATION` | FAIL | A Bug was opened. |
