# C3 — Gate approver recorded

**Lane:** System · **Owner:** Hector (commitment gate) · **Evidence type:** Deterministic

## What it checks

At the commitment gate (Done Specifying → In Implementation), the `Spec Approver` custom field is populated with a user who is in the authorized approvers list for that project, and is not the Story's builder.

## Why it matters

Someone is accountable for every commitment. Today that accountability is implicit — which means no one is. C3 makes it explicit: a named human signed off that the 6-yes was met before this Story entered implementation. Over time, as the grader matures, the human review lightens because the grader does more of it. But the approver remains the gate.

## How the grader evaluates it

Deterministic. Reads the `Spec Approver` field value at the commitment transition (via changelog snapshot). Checks against the `authorized_approvers` list in config. Checks that the approver is not the Story's builder.

## Evidence codes

| Code | Verdict | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `APPROVER_RECORDED_AND_AUTHORIZED` | PASS | Authorized approver; not builder. |
| `APPROVER_FIELD_EMPTY` | FAIL | Field empty at gate. |
| `APPROVER_NOT_AUTHORIZED` | FAIL | Approver not in allowed list. |
| `APPROVER_IS_BUILDER` | FAIL | Approver also built the work. |
