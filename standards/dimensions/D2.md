# D2 — Design artifact for non-trivial work

**Lane:** Downstream · **Owner:** Hector · **Evidence type:** Judge + Deterministic

## What it checks

For Stories judged to be non-trivial (cross-system integration, schema change, multi-screen UI flow, regulatory implication, novel architecture), a design artifact link is populated. Trivial work is exempt.

## Why it matters

Complex work without a design artifact becomes tribal knowledge. When the person who built it leaves or is unavailable, the next engineer inherits a black box. Design artifacts are the durability mechanism for non-trivial decisions.

## How the grader evaluates it

- **Judge:** Claude reads the Story and classifies complexity.
- **Deterministic:** `Design Artifact Link` field is checked for content when the judge classifies the Story as non-trivial.

Judge prompt: [`../../prompts/D2.md`](../../prompts/D2.md).

## Evidence codes

| Code | Verdict | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `DESIGN_NOT_REQUIRED_WORK_TRIVIAL` | PASS | Story deemed trivial. |
| `DESIGN_PRESENT_AS_REQUIRED` | PASS | Complex work, artifact linked. |
| `DESIGN_MISSING_FOR_COMPLEX_WORK` | FAIL | Complex work, no artifact. |
