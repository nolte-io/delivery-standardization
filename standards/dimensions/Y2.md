# Y2 — Observable business difference describable

**Lane:** Upstream · **Owner:** Yanna · **Evidence type:** Deterministic + Judge

## What it checks

The Story describes, before work starts, how we will know the delivery worked. A metric, a threshold, and either a measurement location or an observability mechanism.

## Why it matters

Without an observable difference named upfront, we cannot detect outcome drift, cannot close the loop on whether delivery produced impact, and cannot enforce "we're not yes people" post-release. You cannot kill work that isn't working if no one agreed what "working" looks like.

## How the grader evaluates it

Two checks:

1. **Deterministic:** Story description contains a `## Observable Impact` section with content.
2. **Judge:** Claude confirms the statement contains a metric, a threshold, and a measurement point.

Judge prompt: [`../../prompts/Y2.md`](../../prompts/Y2.md).
