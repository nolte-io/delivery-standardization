# D9 — Story defect rate

**Lane:** Downstream · **Owner:** Hector · **Evidence type:** Deterministic

## What it checks

Per-Story: zero Story Defects raised during validation. One or more = fail. Scope-evolution defects (classified via U9) are excluded from this count — they are a spec-quality signal, not a delivery-quality signal.

## Why it matters

Story Defects are the signal that delivered work did not match agreed AC. A pattern of defects points at either under-specification upstream or incomplete execution downstream. The grader separates these via U9 classification; D9 reports the pure delivery-quality number.

## How the grader evaluates it

Deterministic. Counts linked Story Defects per Story, excluding those classified as scope-evolution by U9.

## Evidence codes

| Code | Verdict | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `ZERO_DEFECTS` | PASS | No in-contract defects. |
| `DEFECTS_PRESENT` | FAIL | One or more in-contract defects. |
